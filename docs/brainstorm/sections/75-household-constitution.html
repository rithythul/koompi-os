The Household Constitution

Futures Privacy

Your memory entries and approval decisions graduate into law: a plain-language constitution — in Khmer or English — that governs what the AI may never do, must always do, and must ask about first. You amend it like a constitution, not a settings panel: changes that lower a guardrail wait out a 72-hour cooling-off, so the 2am version of you cannot overrule the person who wrote Article 3. Families draft one together; your children inherit a starter constitution and amend it at majority.

Law, not settings KOOMPI

Settings are toggles nobody reads. A constitution is plain language you wrote, in your own register of Khmer, enforced mechanically by koompi-egress and the approval layer. The document the AI obeys is the document you can read aloud.

Cooling-off protects you from yourself

Any amendment that weakens a protection waits 72 hours before taking effect. Strengthening is instant. The asymmetry is the point — impulse can add guardrails but never remove them.

The scaling answer for App-less Computing

Per-call modals cannot survive a ten-year shift to intent-level computing. Graduating repeated approvals into articles keeps consent meaningful as the AI's autonomy grows — you govern, rather than supervise.
