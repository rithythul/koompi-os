Data Sovereignty

Data 1.x

A 1.x privacy panel that makes every app's data access visible and revocable. For any installed app you see which scopes it holds, when each was granted, and a one-tap revoke; alongside it is the local log of consent receipts — a per-device record of every share you approved. Receipts live on your machine; the optional Selendra ledger (ADR-0009) stores only tamper-proof proofs that a consent happened, never the data itself.
