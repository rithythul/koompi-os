Aging With Dignity

Futures 2.x

The buildable piece is a set of accessibility scaling profiles, targeted at 2.x. You pick a profile — or nudge individual axes — and the whole shell scales: text size, UI density, click-target area, contrast, plus a simplified mode that reduces on-screen options. Every axis is user-set and reversible in one step. The OS does not silently infer decline and never watches you to guess a profile — you choose it, or you don't.

Profiles are user-set and reversible; the OS does not observe your motor precision, vision, or recall to auto-adjust. No behavioral inference of "decline" crosses the egress gate, because none is produced.
