Corrections outrank inference KOOMPI

A user-asserted fact is written to koompi-agent-memd with a precedence: asserted flag. When a future model update or a fresh inference disagrees, it cannot silently overwrite what you told it — the correction wins, and the conflict is surfaced, not buried. This is value #6 made durable: corrections compound into a more honest system over a lifetime.

Calibration, not confidence theatre

macOS, Windows 11 Copilot, and GNOME present every AI answer with the same flat certainty. KOOMPI shows a real meter and, on a bad streak, plots stated confidence vs measured accuracy. "Recalibrate" acts on that drift — it raises the proactive threshold so the OS earns back the right to speak up.

Undo survives the daemon

A reversed proactive action isn't toast state that vanishes on dismiss. The undo receipt is persisted, so a wrongly-held OTP or a misfiled note stays restorable across a reboot or a koompi-agent-memd restart — for the receipt's full TTL, not just until you look away.

Failure stays on-device

Confidence scoring, the correction loop, and the hallucination bundle all run locally. The report defaults to ~/.local/share/koompi-ai/reports/ and is never auto-sent — even your AI's mistakes are your sovereign data, shared only by an explicit, reviewable choice.
