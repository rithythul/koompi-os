Lifetime OS

Futures 1.x

The buildable kernel here is the legacy preset of the Lifeboat export engine. The engine is v1; the legacy preset lands at 1.x. It packages a person's local archive — documents, decisions, and relationship notes — into an open, human-readable bundle with an index.html that reads on any machine without KOOMPI. Same engine, same format as the other presets; legacy is the tuning aimed at inheritance and long-term readability.

One open bundle, no lock-in: your descendants can read it on any machine. The legacy preset differs from snapshot/refugee/exit only in what it includes and how it lays out the human-readable index.
