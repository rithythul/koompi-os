A relationship, not a product cycle KOOMPI

The OS grows with one person across decades. It remembers who you were at every age and presents your life as eras you can revisit — continuity no cloud account, reset every few years, can offer.

No lock-in, ever

The legacy export is open and human-readable. A fifty-year self can be carried to any machine — or handed to the people who outlive you. Sovereignty includes inheritance.

Ceremony at the thresholds

Major life transitions deserve more than a settings change. The OS marks them, seals the chapter, and adjusts — treating a life as something to honor, not just data to store.
