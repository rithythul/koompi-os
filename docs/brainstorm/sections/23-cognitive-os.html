Cognitive OS

AI 1.x

The "cognitive modes" idea ships as a concrete 1.x feature: Profiles presets. Rather than the OS inferring your mental state, you pick a preset — Deep Work, Meeting, Creative — and the shell switches layout, notification posture, and power profile in one action. There is no cognitive-state sensing and no cadence inference here; the reshaping is user-selected and explicit. This section is now a pointer: the buildable form of this idea lives in §31 Profiles.

Realized as Profiles presets — one tap, no inference
⌖ DEEP WORK
Bar collapses to a thin line · notifications held · single workspace
◎ MEETING
Comms mode · mic + notes up · screen-share guard on
✦ CREATIVE
Spatial canvas · references fanned out · free-tiling unlocked

Each preset is a saved shell posture the user invokes. See §31 Profiles for the schema, the switch mechanism, and the naming boundary against the isolation Subsystem.

Shelved

The "OS reads your cognitive state and reshapes your mind" framing — fatigue detection, cadence-based mode inference, automatic thought capture — stays on the horizon shelf (horizon.md); Profiles ships the switching, not the sensing.
