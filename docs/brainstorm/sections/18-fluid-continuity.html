State of mind, not just files KOOMPI

The capsule carries the live AI context window, draft buffers, and process state — so the thought continues, not just the documents. You resume the sentence, not the folder.

Mesh, not cloud

Devices discover each other on your local network or via an encrypted peer link. The capsule is end-to-end encrypted and never persisted on a third-party server.

Fork vs continue

Continuity is a choice, not an ambush. You decide whether to inherit the desktop's mental state or branch a clean one — the original session waits intact on the other device.
