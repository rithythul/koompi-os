Conversations With Your Past Self

Futures 2.x

The buildable piece, targeted at 2.x+, is a belief-diff view over your own memory history. Because the Memory Lifecycle timestamps a contradiction instead of overwriting it, both the old and new versions of a recorded belief survive. The view picks a topic and shows how your notes on it changed over time — the earlier position, the turning point, the reversal — each entry a real dated record you can reopen. It diffs what you wrote; it does not speak for you or synthesize "what you would say".

Contradictions make the diff possible

The Memory Lifecycle preserves reversed beliefs as timestamped records instead of overwriting them. That one rule is what lets a belief diff exist at all — most systems silently keep only your latest opinion.
