Contradictions are the feature KOOMPI

The Memory Lifecycle preserves reversed beliefs as timestamped records instead of overwriting them. That one rule makes belief diffs, decadal reviews, and an honest history of your mind possible — most systems silently keep only your latest opinion.

The only complete witness

Friends knew eras of you; the OS saw the whole arc. Fifty years of selves, queryable in plain language — and because it never crosses the egress gate, the only person who can interrogate your past is the one who lived it.
