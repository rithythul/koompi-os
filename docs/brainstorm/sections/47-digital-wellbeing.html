Care, not surveillance Local

Every signal — screen time, focus score, posture, light — is computed by on-device daemons and stored in the user's encrypted vault. Nothing is uploaded, not even anonymized. There is no "wellbeing cloud" to leak.

The AI advises, you decide

Unlike Screen Time or Digital Wellbeing, KOOMPI imposes no defaults and never locks you out. Goals are user-set, dismissals are honored, and persistent dismissal retunes the goal instead of escalating the nag.

Honest attribution

Focus scores always carry a plain-language "why" and a link to the raw signals — and admit uncertainty ("I'm not certain — this is a correlation"). No opaque productivity grade. Transparent AI is reviewable AI.

Designed to age Lifetime

A child profile graduates to self-management at 16; an aging user's eye-break cadence can lengthen and reminders enlarge. Decades of wellbeing history stay portable via Selendra identity — the OS grows old with you, never against you.
