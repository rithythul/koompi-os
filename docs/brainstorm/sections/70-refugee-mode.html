Refugee Mode

Futures 1.x

Refugee mode is the refugee preset of the Lifeboat export engine, landing at 1.x. Your home directory is already a portable encrypted volume via systemd-homed; the refugee preset seals that home plus your keys and trust postures into a single encrypted file, small enough for one microSD, unlocked by one passphrase you carry in your head. Any KOOMPI machine can import it. The engine is v1; this preset is a 1.x deliverable.

What we refuse to ship honest refusal
No duress PIN, no decoy, no hidden volume. KOOMPI does not ship a duress passphrase that unlocks a fake life. A decoy volume is forensically visible, and a discovered one turns "I have nothing" into a provable lie — escalating coercion into an obstruction charge (cut for the §37/§41 reasons). We refuse the theater rather than sell a false sense of safety.
The honest protection is absence, not concealment: before you carry the card, the machine's home can be crypto-erased so an inspection finds a genuinely empty device — because that is all that exists on it.
✦ Import onto any KOOMPI machine
A borrowed or secondhand KOOMPI machine accepts the card and the passphrase, and systemd-homed activates your home in place. No network, no account, no server.
koompi-restore import /dev/mmcblk0
├─ passphrase verified · argon2id · 4.1s
├─ integrity 38 GB · all chunks intact

Optionally the card's Merkle root was anchored to Selendra before departure (ADR-0009), so you can later prove the restored home is the unaltered original. The chain holds a fingerprint, never the data.

The refugee preset seals your systemd-homed volume, keys, and postures into one microSD unlocked by one memorized passphrase. No cloud account that can be frozen, subpoenaed, or geo-blocked stands between you and your own home.

Absence, not concealment

Protection is genuine emptiness, not a decoy. koompi-restore can crypto-erase the device's home so an inspection finds the truth: there is nothing on it. We refuse to ship hidden-volume theater that can get someone hurt.
