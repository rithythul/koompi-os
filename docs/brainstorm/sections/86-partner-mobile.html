Pocket rules — the small details
▸ Lock screen shows that a consent waits — never the Approve button. Approval exists only inside the unlocked stream.
▸ Hold, don't tap — commit-class approval is a held fingerprint: consent and identity in one motion, no accidental yes.
▸ Share-sheet is a door — share from any app into a thread; the mobile twin of Files attaching context.
▸ Earbuds mode — replies spoken in formal-register Khmer TTS; usable on a moto.
▸ Battery is the mobile UHD 620 — heavy thinking defers to the home machine, and the phone says what it deferred.
Honest limits on borrowed ground
1 · A companion app on Android cannot hardware-guarantee the layer rule — overlay attacks exist there. Mitigated (FLAG_SECURE, overlay detection), but the true guarantee arrives only with a KOOMPI-controlled mobile build.
2 · The pocket cache is a second copy of some memory — StrongBox-sealed, remotely revocable from the Vault, covered by Refugee mode. A copy named is a copy governed.
3 · Tier handoffs are visible: mesh → tunnel → pocket brain each announce themselves. No tier change is ever silent.
Now: the Android companion (Connect grows into this — stream, capsules, camera-translate, consent cards, wallet ceremony) · Next: the classroom tablet build, KOOMPI's natural hardware play · Horizon: KOOMPI Mobile, where the composer is the first thing your thumb touches when the phone wakes.
Mobile is where this design was born KOOMPI

The desktop Partner Window asks users to unlearn the address bar; the phone asks nothing — composer-at-bottom is already the native grammar of a billion thumbs. The boldest bet on the desktop is the obvious choice in the pocket, which is strong evidence the bet is right.

Your house is your cloud

Siri's brain lives in Cupertino; your partner's brain lives in your living room. The phone is a thin window onto the machine you own — direct over mesh, tunneled when away, honestly smaller when offline. Sovereignty doesn't stop at the desk.

The ceremony got better on glass

A held fingerprint on the consent card fuses agreement with identity — stronger than any desktop click and gentler than any banking app's PIN dance. KHQR payments, form submissions, signatures: the same sentence-first ceremony, now with skin in the loop.
