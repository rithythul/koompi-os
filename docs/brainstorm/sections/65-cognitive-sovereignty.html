Cognitive Sovereignty

Futures 1.x

The concrete piece is local, on-device usage statistics — a 1.x extension of Digital Wellbeing (§47). The OS counts what you already do on the machine: time per app, how often a task was done by hand versus delegated to the assistant. It computes the summary locally and shows it only to you. No score crosses the egress gate, and the OS never acts on the numbers — it just makes them visible if you look.

Local by construction

Usage stats are counted and summarized on-device and gated behind the egress boundary like the rest of §47's wellbeing data. There is no server to send them to and no product incentive to.

Shelved

The "sovereignty of mind" framing — per-capability dependence dials, atrophy hand-back prompts, and an assistant that offers refresher weeks — stays on the horizon shelf (horizon.md); 1.x ships the numbers, not the intervention.
