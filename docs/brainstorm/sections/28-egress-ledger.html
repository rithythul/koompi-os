Append-only BLAKE3 chain Audit

Each ledger entry hashes its predecessor. Any deletion or modification breaks the chain and is immediately detected — the OS alerts on next boot if chain integrity fails.

App-level firewall enforcement

The egress firewall intercepts all outbound connections. Apps without a granted network rule are blocked by default. "Blocked" entries in the ledger represent intercepted attempts.

Selendra attestation for legal proof

The ledger can be anchored to the Selendra blockchain — producing a timestamped, publicly verifiable proof of your data practices without revealing ledger contents.
