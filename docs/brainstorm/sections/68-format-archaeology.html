PDF/A thinking, applied to a whole life KOOMPI

Archivists solved this for documents; KOOMPI applies it to everything you'll ever make. A format-migration daemon walks the episodic store during idle sleep tiers, transcribing endangered formats forward — a perfect T3 job, costing you nothing.

Originals are sacred

Transcription is addition, never replacement. The 2031 .docx sits beside its .odt and .md descendants forever, checksummed, in case a future reader can do better than ours did. Forgetting is ranking, not deletion — for formats too.

Era-correctness is already the precedent

koompi-assistantd tags every vector with the embedding model that made it, so old memories are recalled with their era's understanding. Format archaeology extends the same discipline from meaning to bytes.
