Soul as data, brain as hardware KOOMPI

Cloud assistants reset their relationship with you at every model launch. KOOMPI stores everything that makes the assistant yours outside the weights — so upgrades change capability, never identity.

Era tags all the way down

The same discipline the Memory Architecture applies to vectors — every embedding tagged with its embed_model_id — applies to whole models. Retired weights are archived, not deleted, so any era of your past stays readable by the mind that wrote it.

Succession is a ceremony

Replacing the brain of a fifty-year companion deserves more than a package update. The briefing, the verification conversation, the user's explicit "still me" — continuity is checked by the person it belongs to.
