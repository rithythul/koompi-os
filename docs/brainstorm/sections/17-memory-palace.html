The right to forget

Forgetting is three verbs, not one: Suppress quiets a memory, Seal locks it until a future date, Destroy erases it cryptographically after a waiting period — see Ceremonial Forgetting. Destruction leaves a tombstone, never a copy: the AI is told the memory existed and is gone — it cannot reconstruct it. Sovereignty includes the power to erase.

Only possible because it's local

A cloud AI cannot legally or ethically hold a decade of your inner life. A local one can — the recall surface and the privacy guarantee are the same machine.
